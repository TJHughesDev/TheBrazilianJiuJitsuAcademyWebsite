What Is BJJ?

Brazilian
Jiu Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a grappling-based martial art that focuses on ground fighting and submission holds. Rooted in technique over strength, BJJ is one of the most effective self-defence systems in the world — and one of the most rewarding sports you can practice.

At the TBJJA in Market Drayton, we welcome everyone — whether you want to get fit, learn to defend yourself, compete at a high level, or simply try something new. With classes for adults and children from age 6, there's a place for everyone on the mat.

What We Offer

Our Classes

A range of BJJ classes for adults and children, catering to all skill levels — from complete beginners to seasoned competitors.

Adults

Adult Classes

Dynamic training for all skill levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, our adult classes have a place for you.

Gi

Gi Classes

Traditional BJJ with the kimono. Master the technical, chess-like game of grips, sweeps, and submissions. The gi slows the pace and rewards precise technique over brute strength.

  • All skill levels welcome
  • Gi available to borrow
  • Focus on technique & control
No-Gi

No-Gi Classes

Fast-paced grappling without the gi. No-Gi BJJ focuses on speed, clinch work, and athleticism — popular with MMA practitioners and those who prefer a more dynamic style.

  • Rash guard & shorts
  • Dynamic and athletic
  • Great MMA crossover
Beginners

Beginners Classes

Never trained before? Our beginners classes are the perfect starting point. Step-by-step instruction in a supportive environment — no experience or fitness required whatsoever.

  • Zero experience needed
  • Structured curriculum
  • Small, supportive classes
